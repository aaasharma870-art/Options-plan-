CONTEXT
=======================================================================

Sensor C tags every attached bot with BLACKOUT on days where major
scheduled economic events are occurring. The three events blocked:
- FOMC Meeting
- CPI Release
- NFP (Non-Farm Payrolls) Release

These are the three events most likely to cause intraday volatility
spikes that blow out short-premium positions. Every trading bot reads
the BLACKOUT tag and refuses entry when present.
